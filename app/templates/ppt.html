The Challenge

Forecasting in a Complex Market

Financial markets are notoriously complex. Our goal was to build a model that moves beyond simple trends to understand the deep, non-linear patterns that truly drive market behavior, delivering a robust and reliable forecasting tool.

Project Evolution

From Insight to Impact: Key Upgrades

Based on direct feedback, we implemented two crucial upgrades that fundamentally elevated the project's capabilities and potential.

  • Architectural Leap: LSTM to Transformer
    Upgraded the AI core to a powerful Transformer model to better capture long-range market dynamics.
  • Expanded Data Horizon to August 2025
    All data sources—Technicals, Fundamentals, and News—were extended to August 2025 for enhanced relevance.

Technology & Architecture

A Modern, Scalable Foundation

The system is built on a powerful stack, flowing from data ingestion to a FastAPI backend that serves live predictions. This ensures both high performance and maintainability.

  • Core Technologies: Python, PyTorch, FastAPI
  • AI & Sentiment: Transformer Model, FinBERT

The Data Engine

A Holistic Market View (2015 to 2025)

Our model's key advantage is its ability to synthesize three distinct data pillars, providing a comprehensive and nuanced understanding of market forces.

  • Technical Data: Historical prices and advanced volatility indicators.
  • Fundamental Data: Key company financials like P/E Ratio and Book Value.
  • Sentiment Data: News articles analyzed by FinBERT for daily market mood.

The Result

Exceptional Performance Achieved
